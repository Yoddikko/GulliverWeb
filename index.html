How It Works

1

Connect iPhone

Plug your iPhone into your Mac via USB. Enable Developer Mode on the device.

2

Authorize Tunnel

Gulliver uses Apple's CoreDevice tunnel to establish a secure developer connection.

3

Pick a Location

Search for any place on the map or enter coordinates. Pinpoint where you want to be.

4

Apply & Stay Connected

Click apply. The spoof stays active as long as your Mac remains connected via USB.
